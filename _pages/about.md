My name is Boyue Guo (郭泊岳), a PhD candidate in [Univeristy of Science and technology of China (USTC)](https://www.ustc.edu.cn). Now I'm working with [Fei Yan](https://yanfei-sky.net) on exoplanet.

My research focuses on exoplanet atmospheres, particularly the characterization of hot and ultra-hot giant planets using high-resolution spectroscopy. I am interested in probing atmospheric compositions, thermal structures, atmospheric dynamics, and elemental pattern through transit and thermal emission observations, as well as atmospheric retrieval techniques. My work aims to improve our understanding of the formation, evolution, and physical processes of exoplanetary atmospheres.
